Donate

Mission Bit runs courses that are free to high school students. It costs $500 to run a course for a student each semester. Help keep our courses free to the community by making a tax-deductable contribution today. Any amount helps support the expansion of coding education to San Francisco Bay Area public school students.

For more information about our funding priorities or strategic plan, contact Mission Bit CEO Stevon Cook at stevon@missionbit.com.
