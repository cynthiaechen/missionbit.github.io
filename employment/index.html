Employment

Summer Internships

We place Mission Bit students in summer internships at tech companies and offered paid stipends for students taking part in our leadership development program. Only students that take a semester course with Mission Bit are eligible for paid opportunties. We will be giving more details about paid opportunities during our Spring 2016 course.

Becoming a Software Engineer

In partnership with Mission Bit, Hack Reactor is running a 60 hour coding course called Code Ramp for SFUSD High School Seniors. The course will prepare you for enrollment in Hack Reactor, a coding school that creates software engineers.

Courses will start in January or February depending on the site location. Each course will run Monday through Thursday from 4pm to 7pm over the course of five weeks. Once you are accepted, we will email you the exact dates. 100% attendance is mandatory and critical to your success in the program.
